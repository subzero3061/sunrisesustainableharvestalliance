Vision, Mission, and Values

Vision

To become a leading catalyst for sustainable agriculture and economic empowerment in Papua New Guinea.

Mission

To empower youth and communities through sustainable farming practices, entrepreneurship, and market access, fostering economic growth and environmental stewardship.

Core Values

  • Integrity

    We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical practices in all our endeavors.

  • Innovation

    We encourage creativity and embrace innovative solutions in agriculture and business development.

  • Community

    We value collaboration, inclusivity, and respect for diverse communities.

  • Sustainability

    We are committed to promoting sustainable practices that preserve natural resources and promote long-term viability.

  • Empowerment

    We empower individuals and communities to achieve economic independence and self-reliance.
